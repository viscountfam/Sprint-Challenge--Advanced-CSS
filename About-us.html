About Us

Our magazine started out as the passion project of our creator Andy Moon on August of 1983 in Roswell, New Mexico. Andy and his friends had always been in love with space and ideas about the unknown and after a particular exciting conversation about space they decided to start a magazine. A few months late the first issue of Spacewalkers was published and distributed to the public. Since that day the magazine has continued to grow as more and more people became infected by the staff's enthusiam.

Our goal

While visiting another planet is something that most of us wil only dream about in our lifetimes. Our goal at Spacewalkers is to make your drams of space as detailed as possible by providing you with all the latest info coming out of the vibrant astronomy community around the world.

People always say that the world is a small place. Here at Spacewalkers we want to make the universe a small place too.

The Staff

The staff here at Spacewalkers is a diverse one that cover many backgrounds and beliefs, we are all united in our goal and the passion for astronomy and other sciences.

We are always striving to improve and make our newspaper the best is can be. Our motto is Meliora which is Latin for ever better. If reading a little about our magazine has made you interested in reading it, please click the sign up button below to sign up for a subscription.
